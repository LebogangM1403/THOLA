How It Works

  1. Sign Up & Get Verified

    Create your account using your South African ID and phone number. Get your THOLA Verified Badge.

  2. Post Your Skill or Goods

    Tell the community what you can do or what you are selling.

  3. Connect & Negotiate

    Find what you need, make an offer, and agree on a fair price — safely.

  4. Build Your Reputation

    Every transaction earns you a star rating. Your reputation is your currency.
